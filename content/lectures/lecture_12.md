## Volunteered Geographic Information (VGI)
- Coined by Michael Goodchild (2007)
- Citizens as censors (distributed)
- Aggregate into GIS (centralized)

## Types of VGI
<ul>
<li class="fragment"> Crowd Sourcing and Social Media </li>

</ul>
